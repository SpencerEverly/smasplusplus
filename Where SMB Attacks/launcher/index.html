Since July 14th, 2010, this remake episode has been done in rememberance of the original version of Where SMB Attacks. All criticism and hate may be directed, but this episode feels special to me in many ways. That's why I'm not gonna go back and reedit levels. Thanks for understanding, and enjoy playing Spencer Everly History.


This is the version of Where SMB Attacks that is preincluded with SMAS++.

When Mario was trying to stop Larry from infiltrating Princess Peach's castle, Larry casts a spell to randomize the world! It is up to Mario to save himself, and beat his way out of the randomness!


For the credits of this episode, refer to the "_CREDITS.txt" file in the SMAS++ episode folder.
